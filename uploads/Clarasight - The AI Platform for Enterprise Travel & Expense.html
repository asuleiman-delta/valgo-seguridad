    Questions? Answers.

    Your most frequently asked questions, all in one place. If you don’t see what you need, reach out to us.

    What is Clarasight?

    Clarasight is the AI-native operating system for corporate travel and expense, turning fragmented data into clear decisions, governed workflows, and measurable program outcomes.

    Is Clarasight a replacement for our TMC, booking tool, expense platform, or card?

    No. Clarasight works with your existing stack, connecting travel, expense, card, HR, and business systems to unify data and improve visibility, control, and execution.

    How does Clarasight work?

    Clarasight connects your systems, builds a unified travel and expense model, and lets teams ask questions, surface insights, and trigger actions through guided workflows.

    Is Clarasight just another analytics dashboard?

    No. Clarasight goes beyond reporting by helping teams act on insights with forecasting, approvals, policy enforcement, vendor workflows, and decision-ready outputs.

    Can we trust AI in an enterprise travel program?

    Yes. Clarasight is built for human-led decision-making, with AI supporting analysis and execution inside defined controls, permissions, and auditable workflows.

    Will Clarasight fit our existing environment?

    Yes. Clarasight is designed for complex enterprise environments and integrates with existing systems, helping teams reduce manual work without a rip-and-replace.
